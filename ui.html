    • Treezu

    Treezu is an AllTrails app remake with more nature-oriented colors. The app feature subtle mixtures of greens and using white as the main font color for text. Designs include a login page with a background image that will play as a video in the live edition and a main menu page with local picks and popular locations sections.

    AllTrails color scheme seemed a bit muted to me so I decided to add gradients to make the page more lively.

    Orbitir

    Orbitir is a space traveling app. The objective was to create an app that resembles a normal flight traveling layout but with more futuristic features. The main page consists of a popular planets section with details under about availability, the rocket used, and how long the journey will take.

    At the bottom of the page is another option that lets the user schedule an orbit of the Earth. The main color is a beige to signify a more luxurious interface.

    Feast

    Feast is a food delivery app that offers it's own unique look. The icons and colors create a mix of retro and modern. The app also includes a reservations tab that differs from other known delivery applications.

    I left the app in Dark Mode to highlight the orange colors. I surveyed a few people and found that a darker background with subtle neon colors makes the food look more appetizing. This is highlighted in the main Login Page.
