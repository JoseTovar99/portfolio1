    • Mortgage Calculator

    This project involves a simple calculator that calculates your mortgage loan. All you simply have to do is input the price, percent of interest rate, and the length of the loan. It's a simple and intuitive calculator that's meant to be quick and efficient

    When you calculate the mortgage loan, a calculation appears at the bottom in the Results section. It will show the estimated monthly payment, total payment, and the total interest for the loan. It's very straightforward and can easily get results for multiple homes at a quick rate.
