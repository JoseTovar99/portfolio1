    • Speed Maniac

    Speed Maniac was one of my favorite games, mainly due to my love for racing games. The game was created using C# and Unity3D. It was finally published in Itch.io, a site for indie game developers. The players traverses through a retro city and moon terrain filled with flaming spikes.

    View Live

    I tried creating a nostalgic feeling of a retro arcade game with the assets implemented. There's small animations that occur when the truck accelerates as well as the animated fire. Future plans for the game include implementing multiple maps and movable traps rather than static ones.
